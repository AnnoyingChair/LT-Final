Paddock to plate

The journey of how eggs got from the farm to your lunchbox.

Eggs are used in many foods as a good source of protein and can be cooked in diverse ways such as poached,fried, boiled and scrambled.The journey of an egg when a hen is born. The hen is unable to lay eggs for 6 months after its birth while its a chick. After 6 months, when the chick becomes a mature chicken, the hen starts laying eggs.A hen can lay around one egg per day, as it takes 24-26 hours for an egg to be made.The eggs are collected by the famrers(and washed ) all go through candling testing(shining a light to check for any veins). Aftr this they are sorted by shape, weight and size to its appropriate place. Its then stamped with an expiry date, then bought by retailers(like Woolies or Coles). There it is sold to customers.
