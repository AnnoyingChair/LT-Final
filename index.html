Welcome To BalancedPlate

A website designed for adolescents seeking a healthy balanced diet that is in line with The Australian healthy eating guidelines.Here, you will find information about each of the 6 nutrients and the daily requirements for an adolescent aged 14-18.This website hopes that all adolescents can understand and put into practise Australian Health guidlines regarding a balanced diet. You will also find a delicious recipe for a healthy lunch and Learn how one of the ingredients used in the recipe gets to us from the farm. Here begins your journey to having a happier,healthier and balanced plate.
